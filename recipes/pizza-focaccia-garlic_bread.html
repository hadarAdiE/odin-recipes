Ingredients

For the Dough

  • White plain flour 500 g (dont you dare use whole-weat)
  • 2Tb Honey
  • Some porsean of sourdough (any will do, as long as its not above 100 g)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • unsalted butter(50 g), melted
  • 1 egg
  • Â¼ cup of fine olive oil
  • Water 250 g

For the rest

  • mouzarella cheese
  • pearede tommatos and garlic with some olive oil i, e. tommato paste
  • Olive oil with Crushed Garlic - i, e. olive oil gralic

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together all of the Ingredients for the dough.
  2. Let the dough rest until it doubles its size, meanwhile preheat your oven to 180 degrees
  3. Once the dough is ready, spread it out with your hands (NOT WITH A ROLLING PIN!)to a backing pan lines with some backing sheet.
  4. Spread the tommato paste onto the dough. spread some olive oil garlic onto the edges of the dough.
  5. Once the PFGB is half baked (about 30 min) add some mouzarella on and topping of your choice, add some more olive oil garlic to the edges and put back the PFGB inside the oven
  6. The final baking should take about 20 min more. Once the PFGB is ready (you know when its ready when cheese and belly start bubbling).
  7. Take it out! Add moreeee olive oil garlic! Put the thing in your mouth nowwwww!!
