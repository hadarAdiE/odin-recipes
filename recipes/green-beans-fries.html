Green Beans Fries

You must think Whaat? Green beans as fries?! Give me the real deal!
This is an exception my friends, This crunchy in the outside and owy gooey in the inside has it all! You didn't think I'll leave you with something healthy?!

What can be better than easy munch made with no effort!

Ingredients

  • Frozen green beans
  • Oil (I prefer olive oil)
  • Salt, pepper and spices of your choice

Steps

  1. Mix the green beans with oil, spices and put it straight to the oven!
